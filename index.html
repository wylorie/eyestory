How it works

  1. Click "Start Eye Tracking" and allow camera access.
  2. Look at any word tile; it will highlight when targeted.
  3. Blink once to select a highlighted tile. Select 3 words.
  4. Activate "Generate Story" with a blink to create it.
  5. Use Play/Pause/Stop to hear the story via text-to-speech.
